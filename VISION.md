# Idea Bank — Vision

*Written by: Overseer instance (~/) in collaboration with Diego*
*Date: 2026-07-01*

---

## Why This Exists

Good ideas arrive at bad times. They surface mid-session, mid-build,
mid-conversation — when there's no room to develop them and no safe place
to put them. Without a capture system, they get lost. With one that costs
too much friction to use, they also get lost.

The Idea Bank solves for both: zero friction at capture, organic structure
at scale.

---

## The Founding Principle: Structure Emerges, It Isn't Imposed

Most idea systems fail because they demand categorization at the moment
of capture — the worst possible time to make filing decisions. This system
inverts that:

1. **Capture first, always.** An idea goes into `IDEA-LOG.md` in under a
   minute, tagged loosely, and the current session resumes.

2. **Structure emerges from the log.** When enough ideas accumulate, patterns
   become visible. A pattern becomes a theme folder. A theme with enough
   ideas becomes a project. The system builds itself from what actually
   matters, not from what seemed like it would matter at the start.

3. **The log is always authoritative.** `themes/` and `by-project/` are
   views into the log, not replacements for it. An idea that's never filed
   is still fully captured. An idea that's never captured is lost.

---

## The Recursive Design

This is a tool for building a system that builds itself. It is, itself,
built using the system. Ideas captured here will include ideas about
improving this capture system — and those ideas, when developed, will
make the next version of this tool better than the current one.

That loop is intentional. It is the same loop that runs through every
project in Diego's ecosystem:

- **devlog-engine** — logs sessions that reveal what devlog-engine needs next
- **claude-journal** — documents work that shows what the journal should become
- **ai-foundations-lab** — builds a course by teaching what the course teaches
- **idea-bank** — captures ideas for improving idea capture

The meta-system improves itself by doing the thing it's designed to do.

---

## What This Becomes

In its current form, the Idea Bank is a flat log with placeholders for
organization. In future forms, it could be:

- A queryable database (via devlog-engine) — "show me all infrastructure ideas"
- A review ritual — periodic sessions where the overseer and Diego triage
  what's accumulated
- A project pipeline — ideas that reach a threshold of specificity get
  promoted to new project folders with CLAUDE.md and VISION.md
- An input to the monitoring system — the monitor notices when the idea
  backlog grows beyond a certain size and surfaces it

None of that needs to be built now. The log is sufficient to start, and
the structure it needs will become clear from using it.
